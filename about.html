Who we are?

Our journey began in 1990 when my father started his woodworking business, driven by his passion for crafting elegant furniture that blends tradition with modern design. Over the years, as industries expanded, skilled local craftsmen faced challenges despite delivering high-quality work. Many of their products, often more affordable than online stores, remain inaccessible to customers due to a lack of visibility. We built this platform to bridge that gap—connecting local furniture makers with buyers who value craftsmanship, affordability, and quality. Sellers can showcase their work, and customers can effortlessly browse and purchase furniture from nearby firms. With a hassle-free buying experience and up to 3 years warranty (T&C apply), we ensure reliability, accessibility, and satisfaction.
